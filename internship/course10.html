Course Overview

This course offers a complete roadmap for IT professionals to understand, deploy, and manage cloud-based solutions. Learn how to leverage cloud services securely and efficiently to drive business transformation. Real-world examples and hands-on labs will ensure you gain practical expertise.

    Implement best practices for secure cloud environments.

    Plan and execute successful cloud migrations.

  • Module 6: Monitoring and Optimization

    Use tools to monitor, scale, and optimize resources.
