Corporate Finance and Valuation

Develop deep expertise in corporate financial management and valuation strategies to drive business success.

Course Overview

This course provides a rigorous foundation in corporate finance principles and advanced valuation techniques. You will explore capital structure decisions, dividend policies, and learn how to value companies using industry-standard methods. Perfect for finance professionals seeking a comprehensive skill set.

Course Modules

  • Module 1: Corporate Finance Fundamentals

    Understand the core concepts of financial management.

  • Module 2: Capital Budgeting

    Analyze investment opportunities and project evaluations.

  • Module 3: Valuation Techniques

    Master DCF, comparables, and precedent transaction analysis.

  • Module 4: Capital Structure & Cost of Capital

    Determine optimal financing strategies and WACC calculations.

  • Module 5: Dividend Policy

    Explore theories and practical approaches to dividends.

  • Module 6: Financial Modeling

    Build robust financial models in Excel for decision-making.
