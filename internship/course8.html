Investment Banking and Capital Markets

Gain the skills to navigate complex financial transactions and drive capital market success with confidence.

Course Overview

This comprehensive course demystifies the world of investment banking and capital markets. You will learn how financial institutions raise capital, structure deals, and advise clients. Through real-world case studies, you'll develop the analytical and strategic skills to excel in a competitive industry.

Course Modules

  • Module 1: Introduction to Investment Banking

    Explore the structure and functions of investment banks.

  • Module 2: Capital Raising Strategies

    Understand debt and equity financing mechanisms.

  • Module 3: Mergers & Acquisitions

    Learn the process and valuation techniques behind M&A.

  • Module 4: Equity Capital Markets

    Examine IPOs, follow-on offerings, and market regulations.

  • Module 5: Debt Capital Markets

    Analyze bonds, syndication, and credit products.

  • Module 6: Risk Management

    Identify and mitigate financial risks effectively.
